NJP RAILWAY STATION / IXB AIRPORT � DARJEELING (75 KILOMETERS / 3 HOURS)

On arrival at Bagdogra Airport/New Jalpaiguri Railway station, our representative will meet you at
the airport/railway station and assist you till your check in process at the Darjeeling hotel(6,950 feet).
Stay overnight at the Darjeeling hotel.





DARJEELING SIGHTSEEING

This morning, you will drive to Tiger Hill (8,400 feet) to chase the down and see the stunning view
of the sun rising and throwing it pinkish glow on the landscapes. After spending some time at the tiger hill, you will embark
on a drive to the hotel via Ghum Monastery, which famed for its 15-feet high statue of "Maitreya Buddha" and Batasia
Loop, famous for its war memorial. Enjoy scrumptious breakfast at the hotel and then go for the city sightseeing tour.
As per the itinerary, today you will be visiting Himalayan Mountaineering Institute, P.N. Zoological Park (Thursday closed),
Tenzing Rock, Tibetan Refugee self-help Centre (Sunday closed), Tea Garden (outer view), Ropeway and Japanese Temple.
Evenings will be free for leisure. Stay and enjoy dinner at Darjeeling.

DARJEELING � GANGTOK (100 KM / 4 HOURS)

.This morning after breakfast, drive to Gangtok (5,500 feet), which is 100 km from Darjeeling
. View of lush green mountains and bubbling brooks will greet you at every turn. In the evening, you
will be transferred to hotel on arrival at Gangtok, acclimatize to the weather, followed by an overnight stay.



  

GANGTOK LOCAL SIGHTSEEING

In the morning, after breakfast, go for a city sightseeing tour. Going by the itinerary, today,
you will be visiting Tashi view point, Ganesh Tok, Hanuman Tok, Flower show, Cottage Industry
and Handicraft Centre, Institute of Tibetology, Dro-dul Chorten, Enchey Monastery & Banjakhri Falls.
Thereafter, you will be transferred to Gangtok hotel for overnight stay and dinner.
