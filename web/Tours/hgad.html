Day 1 - Journey to Khireshwar

Late Night Journey
From your respective destination, board a train to Kalyan (W) ST Bus Depot in the evening
and assemble outside the station by around 0950PM. Following this, the excitement of your trek to
Harishchandragad will begin by around 1000PM as you will be boarding an overnight bus to Khireshwar (via Khubi Phata).





Day 2 - Arrival in Khireshwar

Sunrise View, Kedarling, Konkan Kada
Arriving in the hamlet of Khireshwar by around 12:00AM, attend an intro session from the trek guides
and start hiking towards the sunrise point by around 0450AM. Witness the magical sunrise views from
this point and start trekking towards one of the nearby caves in Harishchandragad.
Your arrival at the caves; at around 0730 in the morning, will be followed by a trek to the Kedarling.
Completing this half an hourï¿½s trek, take enough time to explore the scenic vicinity and fill in yourself
with a much deserved breakfast and a warm cup of tea/coffee by 09:30AM. Post this, spend some leisure
moments and discover the enchanting beauty of Harishchandragad.
At around 01:30PM or lunchtime, munch on a delicious lunch and proceed towards Konkan Kada.
Taking around 2-3 hours, the completion of this exhilarating trek will be followed by your return trek
to the caves, where you will spend overnight after dinner served by 0830 in the evening.



  

Day 3 - Departure from Khireshwar

Trek Ends Here Wake up early in the morning; around 06:30AM, and enjoy your breakfast.
Post this, relax for a while and start trekking towards Khireshwar by around 08:30AM.
As you arrive in Khireshwar, have your lunch and get prepared to depart for Kalyan by
around 12:30PM; expected to reach Kalyan by 04:30PM.
